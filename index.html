— How It Works

Three roles, one
seamless chain.

Whether you farm catfish in Ondo, raise Giant African Snails in Ogun, or source bulk orders for your Lagos restaurant — every transaction flows through a secure three-party system built for you.

🌿

Fish & Snail Farmer

Aquaculture and heliculture farmers onboard via our lightweight PWA. Works on low-bandwidth networks with offline sync support.

  • 1 Register with phone + SMS OTP
  • 2 Complete KYC — admin approves
  • 3 List fish or snails: species, format, price
  • 4 Receive order, confirm pickup via OTP
  • 5 85% of funds auto-released after delivery
🛒

Urban Buyer

Restaurants, hotels, and wholesalers source fresh fish and snails through WhatsApp — no app download required.

  • 1 Message AquaBridge on WhatsApp
  • 2 Get matching fish or snail listings
  • 3 Select and pay via Paystack link
  • 4 Funds held in escrow until delivery
  • 5 Inspect goods, release with OTP
🚚

Logistics Driver

Verified drivers pick up from farms and deliver to urban buyers, earning 10% of every order they complete.

  • 1 Register and get admin-verified
  • 2 Accept available delivery jobs
  • 3 Confirm pickup with seller OTP
  • 4 Transport goods safely to buyer
  • 5 10% payout released on delivery

— Escrow & Payments

Money moves only when
trust is confirmed.

Every fish and snail transaction on AquaBridge is backed by escrow. Funds don't release until the buyer physically inspects and approves the delivery with a one-time PIN.

🔐

Zero-risk for buyers

Your money stays locked until you're satisfied. Raise a dispute anytime before releasing.

⚡

Instant seller payout

Funds split and transferred within minutes of OTP confirmation. No waiting, no friction.

🛡️

Duplicate-proof webhooks

Every Paystack webhook is checked against idempotency keys. No double-charges, ever.
